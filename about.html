About

I'm a student of mathematics and computation, interested in the places where structure meets intuition. I write here to think — not to perform thinking, but to actually do it.

You'll find notes on probability, algorithms, competitive programming, and occasional essays on whatever I'm reading or working through. The writing is slow and, I hope, honest.

I believe that the meditative state — the one where you lose track of time inside a problem — is the most productive state a person can inhabit. Most of what I do is an attempt to get back there.
